Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • ½ cup Forsten Milk

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour three 9-inch round cake pans.
    2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    3. Add the eggs, buttermilk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
    4. Gradually add the boiling water to the batter and stir until the mixture is smooth. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
    5. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cake pans.
    6. Use the remaining frosting to cover the top and sides of the cake.
    7. Chill the cake for at least an hour before serving to allow the frosting to set.

    Pro Tip

    Make the bottom layer of the cake moist with a little milk. Also, don't forget to chill it for a few hours before digging in.
