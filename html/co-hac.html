Cooking Hacks

Hack 1: Quick Vegetable Peeling

Use the edge of a spoon to easily peel ginger or scrape the skin off of vegetables like carrots and potatoes.

Hack 2: Easy Garlic Peeling

Place garlic cloves in a bowl, cover it with another bowl, and shake vigorously. The skins will loosen, making peeling a breeze.

Hack 3: Prevent Overcooking Pasta

Add a pinch of salt and a drop of oil to the boiling water before adding pasta to prevent it from sticking and overcooking.

Hack 4 :Soften Butter Quickly

Grate cold butter using a cheese grater to soften it faster for baking or spreading on bread.
